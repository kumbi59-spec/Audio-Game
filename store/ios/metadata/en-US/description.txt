EchoQuest is an audio-first interactive storytelling platform guided by a live AI Game Master powered by Claude. Every feature works by voice, keyboard, and screen reader — built for blind and sighted adventurers alike.

PLAY YOUR WAY
• Choose from official narrated campaigns or community-created worlds
• Speak your actions out loud or type them — the AI Game Master responds to anything
• Press 1–9 to pick choices, V to speak, I for inventory, Q for quests
• High-quality ElevenLabs narration voices bring every scene to life

FULLY ACCESSIBLE
• Every screen is fully navigable by VoiceOver without a single tap
• ARIA live regions announce all dynamic game events
• Adjustable TTS speed and volume at any time
• Reduced motion mode supported

UPLOAD YOUR OWN WORLD
• Upload a PDF, DOCX, or text file — your rules, lore, and world-building notes
• Claude AI turns it into a fully playable interactive campaign in seconds
• Share your world with the community (Creator plan)

BUILT FOR EVERYONE
EchoQuest started as an accessibility project. We believe great interactive fiction should be available to everyone — whether you're commuting with earbuds, playing on a screen reader, or just prefer audio over text.

SUBSCRIPTION
• Free: 3 official campaigns, browser TTS, basic play
• Storyteller ($9/mo): Unlimited play, premium ElevenLabs voices, Game Bible upload
• Creator ($19/mo): World Builder, community publishing, analytics
• All core accessibility features are always free

Audio narration requires an internet connection. Voice input uses on-device speech recognition when available.
