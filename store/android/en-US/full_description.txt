EchoQuest is an audio-first interactive storytelling platform guided by a live AI Game Master powered by Claude. Every feature works by voice, keyboard, and screen reader — built for blind and sighted adventurers alike.

🎙️ PLAY YOUR WAY
• Choose from official narrated campaigns or community-created worlds
• Speak your actions out loud or type them — the AI responds to anything
• Tap 1–9 to pick choices, use voice commands, or navigate by keyboard
• High-quality narration voices bring every scene to life

♿ FULLY ACCESSIBLE
• Navigate entirely by TalkBack without any visual interaction
• All dynamic game events are announced via accessibility services
• Adjustable TTS speed and volume at any time
• Designed from the ground up for blind and low-vision users

📖 UPLOAD YOUR OWN WORLD
• Upload a PDF, DOCX, or text file with your rules, lore, and world notes
• Claude AI turns it into a fully playable interactive campaign
• Share your world with the community (Creator plan)

💜 BUILT FOR EVERYONE
EchoQuest started as an accessibility project. All core accessibility features are always free — keyboard navigation, voice input, screen reader support, and browser TTS.

🎮 SUBSCRIPTION
• Free: 3 official campaigns, basic play, browser TTS
• Storyteller ($9/mo): Unlimited play, premium voices, Game Bible upload
• Creator ($19/mo): World Builder, community publishing, analytics

Requires internet connection for AI narration. Voice input uses on-device speech recognition.
